* Direction of magnetic field-

  1. Dot Convention:

 

If the current is flowing through a conductor is moving towards the observer and out of the plane of paper, then this direction is indicated by Dot.

If the current is flowing through conductor moving away from the observer and into the plane of paper, then this current direction is indicated by cross.
